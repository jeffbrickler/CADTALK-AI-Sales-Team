# Follow-up Email Template

Use after the decision-gate meeting.

## Subject Options

- `Confirmed next steps for [solution]`
- `[Account] proposal review - decision path`
- `Next steps from today's proposal review`

## Email Body

`Thank you for the time today. Based on the discussion, we aligned that the priority is [business problem/outcome]. The recommended path is [solution/package], with implementation focused on [scope and value].`

`The proposed investment is [investment summary]. The remaining items before approval are [open items].`

`Agreed next steps:`

`1. [Owner] - [action] by [date].`
`2. [Owner] - [action] by [date].`
`3. [Owner] - [action] by [date].`

`If this matches your understanding, the next step is [approval/procurement/signature/kickoff action] by [date].`

## Rules

- Keep it under 150 words when sending to executives.
- Do not re-sell the entire solution.
- Do not use vague phrases like `circle back`, `touch base`, or `review internally` without owner and date.
- Include proposal or agreement link/attachment only if available.
