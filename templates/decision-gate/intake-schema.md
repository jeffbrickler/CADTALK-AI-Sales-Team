# Intake Schema

Use this when the user provides messy notes or asks for a reusable prompt/template.

## Required Context

- Account:
- Industry:
- Meeting date:
- Attendees and roles:
- Decision owner:
- Champion:
- Proposed solution:
- Demo completed: yes/no
- Solution architecture reviewed: yes/no
- Confirmed business problem:
- Current-state impact:
- Success criteria:
- Proposed scope:
- Subscription price:
- Implementation/services price:
- Term:
- Timeline target:
- Implementation owner:
- Procurement/legal process:
- Known objections:
- Desired meeting outcome:

## Optional Context

- Competitors:
- Partner involvement:
- ERP/CRM/PLM/CAD systems:
- Customer quote or phrase:
- Internal deadline:
- Budget source:
- Approval threshold:
- Contracting blockers:
- Security or IT requirements:
- Expansion path:

## Missing Data Handling

If a required item is missing, do not stall. Use one of these:

- `Assumption to verify: economic buyer appears to be [role].`
- `Open risk: decision process is not yet confirmed.`
- `Recommended question: who owns final approval after this meeting?`
